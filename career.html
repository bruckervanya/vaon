Principles & Culture

Vaon's competitive edge is our pioneering workplace culture. Our cultural identity is continuously evolving. We believe new people can and should change how we operate and what we value.

  • Truthful and transparent

    Our workplace culture relies on truthful and transparent communication to ensure the best ideas win out.

  • Diversity

    We champion diversity because it is essential to our ability to think differently.

  • Inclusion

    We cultivate inclusion because we believe people do their best work when they can be their genuine selves.

  • Thoughtful

    We encourage open and honest dialogue and allowing the best thinking to prevail.

  • Growth mindset

    we provide all our employees with the development they need to fulfill their potentials as professionals and people.

  • Collaborative

    Many of our biggest advances grew out of work done across multiple teams.
